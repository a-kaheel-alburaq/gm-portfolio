About Golden Meat


We Provide Best Meat

We are trusted by many clients from all over the country. Golden Meat International Company imports, markets, and distributes frozen, chilled, fresh meat and others in Saudi Arabia. Since its establishment in 2018, the company has expanded its customer base to over 2000 retail, food service, and wholesale establishments. It runs three branches, each of which has a warehouse with temperature control. Golden Meat International Company works with more than 40 suppliers, the majority of whom are well-known on a global scale and have established market existence worldwide.
